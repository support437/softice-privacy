Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Softice Innovations Labs LLC (“Softice,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) develops mobile applications, including Bedtime Story Books. This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use our apps.

1) Information We Collect

We are committed to a child-safe experience and do not require user accounts.

We do not directly collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Contacts or photos
  • User-generated profile data

However, some limited technical data may be processed automatically by platform/service providers to operate and secure the app, such as:

  • Device/app diagnostics
  • Crash logs
  • Performance and stability signals
  • Purchase transaction status (for in-app purchases)

This data is used only for app operation, billing, and reliability.

2) Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Our app is designed for families and children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 directly through in-app forms or account creation.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us directly, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

3) Third-Party Services

Our app may use these services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing (for in-app purchases)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (optional crash diagnostics, if enabled)

These providers may process limited technical data according to their own privacy policies.

4) How Information Is Used

Any limited technical data processed by third parties is used only for:

  • Core app functionality
  • Purchase verification and entitlement handling
  • App security and fraud prevention
  • Crash reporting and bug fixes
  • Performance and stability improvements

We do not sell personal data.

5) Data Sharing

We do not share personal data for advertising or data brokerage. Limited technical information may be processed by service providers listed above solely to deliver app functionality.

6) Data Retention

We do not maintain user account profiles. Where diagnostic logs exist through third-party services, retention is controlled by those providers’ standard retention settings and policies.

7) Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your region, you may have rights to request access, deletion, or correction of data processed about you. Because we do not operate a user-account database, requests are handled to the extent technically possible.

For deletion/support requests, contact us at: support@softiceinnovationslabs.com

8) Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect app integrity and reduce misuse.

9) International Processing

Service providers may process technical data on servers in different countries. By using the app, you acknowledge such processing as needed to operate the service.

10) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above.
