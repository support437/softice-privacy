Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Softice Innovations Labs LLC (“we”, “our”, or “us”) develops mobile applications including Bedtime Story Books. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Our apps are designed for families and children. We do not collect any personal data from users.

  • No names
  • No email addresses
  • No phone numbers
  • No location data
  • No analytics that identify the user

Some anonymized diagnostic data may be processed by Google (Google Play Services) solely for app stability.

2. Children’s Privacy (COPPA)

Our applications do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The app is safe for family use and contains no account system.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing (for in-app purchases)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (optional crash diagnostics)

4. How We Use Information

Minimal data, if processed by third parties, is used strictly for:

  • App functionality
  • Bug prevention
  • Security

5. Your Rights

You may contact us at any time to request deletion of any stored diagnostic logs tied to your device ID.
